Hey there! So, you're looking to host your own live streams? You've come to the right place. I built Livetran to be a straightforward, no-nonsense live streaming server that just works. It's written in Go, so it's fast and lightweight.

Whether you're setting up a stream for a big online event or just tinkering on a personal project, Livetran gives you the tools you need without the headache.

## What's in the Box?

I've packed Livetran with some pretty cool features to make your life easier. Here's the rundown:

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Secure SRT Ingestion" icon="shield-halved">
    Uses SRT for solid, low-latency streaming, even if your internet connection is a bit shaky.
  </Card>
  <Card title="JWT Authentication" icon="key">
    Keeps your streams safe and sound with JWT-based stream keys. No uninvited guests!
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simple REST API" icon="code">
    A dead-simple API to start, stop, and check on your streams.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time Webhooks" icon="webhook">
    Get instant pings on your own servers whenever something happens with your stream.
  </Card>
  <Card title="FFmpeg Transcoding" icon="video">
    Automatically transcodes your stream into HLS format on the fly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cloudflare R2 Uploads" icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    Uploads all your video files straight to R2, so you can scale without breaking a sweat.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
